About the app

DocScan Pro is an Android document scanner. Capture pages with the camera, fine-tune the corners, straighten and filter the result, and save the scan as a PDF or JPG. Optional on-device OCR (Google ML Kit) extracts text from each page so you can copy or export it. Everything runs locally — the app does not maintain a backend or upload your documents anywhere.
