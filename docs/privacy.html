Privacy Policy

Last updated: 10 May 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how DocScan Pro ("the App", "we", "us") handles your information. By installing or using the App you agree to the practices described below.

1. Data we do not collect

DocScan Pro is designed to work entirely on your device. We do not run our own servers, we do not require you to create an account, and we do not transmit your scanned documents to us. The text recognised by the OCR engine, the images you capture, and the documents you save never leave your phone unless you explicitly share them through the system share sheet.

2. Data stored on your device

  • Scanned page images and the SQLite database that catalogs them are stored in the App's private storage.
  • Settings such as your default save format, theme, and whether you have unlocked the lifetime Premium upgrade are stored in shared preferences.
  • OCR text and the layout-preserving block structure used by the clean-document export are stored alongside the page record in the same private database.

3. Camera and storage permissions

The App requests the camera permission so it can show a live preview and capture document scans. It uses Android's app-specific external storage (no broad storage permission is required) to write exported PDF and JPG files into /Android/data/com.docscanar.app/files/. You can revoke either permission at any time through Android Settings.

4. Third-party services

The App integrates with three Google services:

  • Google ML Kit Text Recognition — runs on-device. The model is downloaded once by Google Play Services and processes your images locally.
  • Google Mobile Ads (AdMob) — when ads are enabled, AdMob may collect a limited set of identifiers (advertising ID, coarse network info) for ad delivery and frequency capping. Their handling is governed by Google's Privacy Policy.
  • Google Play Billing — used solely to process the optional lifetime Premium purchase. We never see your payment instrument; the App only receives an "entitled / not entitled" signal from Play.

5. Children

The App is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children.

6. Your choices

  • Delete a document inside the App to remove its image files and database rows from your device.
  • Uninstall the App to remove all of its private storage at once.
  • Disable the camera permission in Android Settings if you no longer want the App to access your camera.

7. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Material changes will be reflected in the "Last updated" date at the top of this page.

8. Contact

Questions or concerns about this policy can be sent to chillocreative@gmail.com.
